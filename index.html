Color Astrology: Dress yourself According to the Color of the Day

We live in a colorful world. If we think for a moment all the colors of the world are over, then the world will be colorless. A single thought of colorless world disturbs the peace of mind. We always want to leave in a colorful world so most of the time we dream of colors. Generally colors are not only pleasant for eyes, but also effective in our lives in many ways. Colors are also use in the treatment of many diseases through color therapy. Color Astrology is also play major role in our life. It help us which color we want to wear at that day, time and events. Here I also write about the color astrology by day. You see here which color is suitable for you and which day. The color astrology according to each day is as follows.

Sunday

Astrologically, Sunday is the day of Sun. Sun indicates the soul of ego, empathy, energy, father, health, king, politics, honor, bravery and medical science. As a result, the Sunday wearing clothes of colors might be bright yellow, light red, pink and orange. It will be effective in blessing you with all the things mentioned above. The jewelry should be used with pink stones or raised color stones, like ruby, garnets and blood stones necklace and bracelet also.

Monday

Monday is the day of Moon. Moon indicates the soul of respect, interest, sleep, mother, power, travels, happiness, wealth and water. For the benefits times, events and things you are advised to wear the cloths of white, silver, light blue and other related shades of colors with silver stone jeweler like pearl jeweler.

Tuesday

Tuesday is the day of ruling planet of Mars. It is indicates the Jupiter signifies power, bravery, courage, younger brother, land movable property and competition. The best wearing dresses color for Tuesday is red and orange colors with red shades jeweler embedded with coral. It will be auspicious for you.
